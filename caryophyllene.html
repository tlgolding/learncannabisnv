Caryophyllene

Peppery, Spicy

About

  • Only terpene that can bind directly to the cannabinoid receptors
  • Has the highest cannabinoid activity of any terpene
  • Oftentimes what dogs smell about cannabis

Effects

  • Soothing/relaxing/calming
  • Euphoric/mood-lifting

Practical Applications

  • Anti-inflammatory/pain reliever
  • Antibacterial/Antifungal/Antimicrobial
  • Pesticide/insect repellant
  • Flavoring for food, candy, gum, etc.
  • Fragrance in perfumes/cosmetics
  • Fragrance in cleaners and industrial fluids

Unique Applications:

  • Dilates blood vessels and increases blood flow
  • Improves cold tolerance
  • Supports the immune system
  • Improves wound healing
  • Influences insulin secretion
  • Could help in treating addiction
