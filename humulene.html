Humulene

Hoppy, Spicy

About

  • Responsible for the distinct hoppy taste in beer
  • Often found with caryophyllene
  • Closely related to caryophyllene - was once called alpha-Caryophyllene

Effects

  • Soothing/calming
  • Sedative

Practical Applications

  • Anti-inflammatory/pain reliever
  • Anticancer
  • Pesticide/Insect repellant

Unique Applications

  • Suppresses appetite

Also Found In:

  • Hops
  • Marsh elders
  • Ginseng
  • Orange tree leaves
  • Tobacco
  • Sunflower leaves
  • Sage
  • Pine trees
  • Ginger
