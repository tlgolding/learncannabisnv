Pinene

Fresh, piney

About

  • Most abundant terpene in nature
  • Counteracts negative side effects of THC like memory loss and sleepiness
  • High levels in turpentine, which has been used as a medicine, solvent, and cleaner for thousands of years

Effects

  • Energetic
  • Focused
  • Euphoric

Practical Applications

  • nti-inflammatory/pain relief
  • Antibacterial/antifungal/antimicrobial
  • Anticancer
  • Pesticide/insect repellant
  • Opens BBB

Unique Applications

  • Topically applied can reduce oil production
  • Helps memory retention and promising for Alzheimer’s
  • Bronchodilator – opens air passageways
  • Inflammation reduction applicable to asthma, bronchitis, MRSA, COPD, Crohn’s, and MS
